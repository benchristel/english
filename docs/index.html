A Grammar of the English language

English (or, in English, Englic or Englalic) is a constructed language that I've been working on since 2023. It's spoken by people who call themselves the Englings or "descendants of angels", and live on the fictional island of Englalond in the northern Atlantic ocean.

In this grammar, I describe, to the best of my ability, how to speak and write English. It is a work in progress and, probably contains many errors and omissions. All such mistakes are, of course, my own (whose else would they be?)
