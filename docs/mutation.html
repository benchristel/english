Mutation

Mutation refers to the way that the initial consonant sound of a word in EnglishLANGNAME changes in certain situations.

Original sound Mutated sound
p b
t d
k g
b v
d dh
g ' (silent)
m v

Examples

  • gwydhtree "tree" → 'wydhtree#LEN
  • mollgethcurse "curse" → vollgethcurse#LEN
  • tavelevil_0 "evil" → davelevil_0#LEN
  • kalennmonth "month" → galennmonth#LEN
  • daesgod "god" → dhaesgod#LEN
  • tarosvanghost "ghost" → darosvanghost#LEN
  • methafinal_0 "last, final" → vethafinal_0#LEN
  • penhead "head" → benhead#LEN

Mutation applies to sounds, not letters. th^th and dh^dh don't mutate.

  • theulpebble "pebble" → theulpebble#LEN.
