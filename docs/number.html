Plurals

The plural of nouns is formed by suffixing -owBLANK#PL. Often, this causes the final consonant to double (in writing).

  • gwydhowtree#PL "trees"
  • tarosvannowghost#PL "ghosts"
  • dweggowduck#PL "ducks"

Nouns also have a collective form similar to the derivation bag → baggage.

  • dwegiodduck#COLL "duckage"

The exact semantics of singular, plural, and collective forms are still under investigation.
