Appositives

An appositive construction is when two noun phrases next to each other refer to the same thing. In EnglishLANGNAME, nouns in an appositive phrase must be joined by tybe_1 "being". As a modifier, tybe_1 is mutated to dybe_1#LEN if, and only if, it immediately follows the noun it modifies.

Aragorn^Aragorn dybe_1#LEN Taranking#CAP 'aof_1#LEN Gondor^Gondor "Aragorn, King of Gondor"
